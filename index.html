Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we are in-network with Aetna, First Health Network, Evernorth, Medicare, Blue Cross Blue Shield, and United Healthcare. If your insurance is not listed, contact us to check eligibility.

What is the cost of counseling services?

The cost varies depending on the type of service you need and your insurance coverage. Through our PharmCo 340B partnership, we also work to significantly lower medication costs. Please contact us directly to discuss our fees and sliding scale options.

How often should I attend sessions?

The frequency of sessions will depend on your individual needs and clinical goals. Some clients benefit from weekly EMDR or CBT sessions, while others may attend bi-weekly, or simply schedule monthly medication management check-ins.
